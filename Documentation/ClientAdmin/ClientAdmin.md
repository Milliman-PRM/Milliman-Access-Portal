## Creating a New Client
1. Press the large "New Client" button on the left side of
the screen. Or, press the small "+" button to the right of the "Filter Clients"
text box. A form should open allowing for client information to be input.

## Editing Client Information
1. Select the client and then press the "Edit Client Details"
button, symbolized by a pencil, in the top-right corner of the form.
1. Select the desired text box and input the updated information.
