Content Publishing

  1. Select the client to which the content will be published in the Client Tree column on the left side of the screen
  2. Click on the plus sign next to the search bar or click on the large “Add Content Item” button
  3. Fill out the Content Item form
    • Field names designated with '*' are required
    • If the content is intended to be reduced, click the Reducible toggle to the "ON" position
    • After the content item is saved, the content type and Reducible status cannot be changed
    • The Description field is for text that will appear on the content item card for all users with access to the content
    • The Notes field is for text that will only appear on the publishing page and can serve as notes for publishing users
    • In the Files area of the form, click on a field for a prompt to upload a file; all are optional except the content file itself
  4. Click “Create Content Item”
  • Processing of publications is performed in the order requested; depending on the size and complexity of the publication, this may take some time
  • The status of the request will be displayed below the content item card on this page

    • Approving a New Content Item for Go-Live

    1. After the Content Item has reached the "Processed" status, click the green checkmark on the Content Item card to review the processed publication
    2. Review and check off each section (if it is correct), and consider the attestation statement. Note that for the FileDownload content type, you can download the file to review it
    3. If something looks incorrect, click Reject to cancel the publication
    4. Click Approve at the bottom of the publication preview to make the content live
