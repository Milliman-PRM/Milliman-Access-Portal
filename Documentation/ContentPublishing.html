Content Publishing

  1. Select a client under the Client Tree column on the left side of the screen
  2. Click on the plus sign next to the search bar or click on the large “Add Content Item” button
  3. Fill out the Content Item form
  4. Click “Create Content Item”

Publishing a New Content Item

  1. After the virus scan has complete, click the green checkmark on the Content Item card to review the publication
  2. Review and check off each section (if it is correct) and click Approve. Note that for FileDownload content types, you can download the file to review it
  3. If something looks incorrect, click Reject to cancel the publication
