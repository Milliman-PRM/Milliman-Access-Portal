Managing Users

expand_more

Creating a New User

  1. Click on the Users tab in the left column
  2. Click the plus sign near the search bar
  3. Fill in user email
  4. Click Create User

Managing User Roles

  1. Click on the Users tab in the left column
  2. Click a user card to manage
  3. Toggle System Permissions and User Settings in the far right section

Managing User Roles at Client Level

  1. Click on the Users tab in the left column
  2. Click a user card to manage
  3. Click on the Clients tab in the center column
  4. Click on the Client card for which the user's roles will be managed
  5. Toggle individual Client/User Roles settings in far right section

Suspending Users

  1. Click on the Users tab in the left column
  2. Click the user card to be suspended
  3. Click the Suspended toggle on the far right to the ON position

Managing Clients

expand_more

Adding A User to a specific Client

  1. Click on the Clients tab in the left column
  2. Click a Client card to be managed
  3. Click on the Users tab in the center column
  4. Click on the plus sign next to the Filter Users... search bar
  5. Enter the user's email address
  6. Click Add User"

Suspending Content

  1. Click on the Clients tab in the left column
  2. Click a Client card to be managed
  3. Click on the Content Items tab in the center column
  4. Choose a content item
  5. Click on the Suspended toggle on the far right under Content Item Details

Increasing the Client Domain Limit

  1. Click on the Clients tab in the left column
  2. Click a Client card to be managed
  3. Click on the Increase Domain Limit button under the Client Information on the right
  4. Fill in the new domain limit, the email of the user that requested the increase, and the reason for the increase
  5. Once the form has been completed, click the UPDATE DOMAIN LIMITS button

Managing Profit Centers

expand_more

Creating a Profit Center

  1. Click on the Profit Center tab in the left column
  2. Click on the plus sign next to the searh bar for Profit Center
  3. Fill in new Profit Center information
  4. Click on Create Profit Center

Adding Authorized Users to a Profit Center

  1. Click on the Profit Center tab in the left column
  2. Click to select a Profit Center to add an authorized user to
  3. Click on the Authorized Users tab in the center column
  4. Click on the plus sign next to the Authorized Users search bar
  5. Input the authorized user's email address
  6. Click on Add User

Viewing Clients in a Profit Center

  1. Click on the Profit Center tab in the left column
  2. Choose a Profit Center to view Client information
  3. Click on the Clients tab in the center column
  4. Click on the Client card to be viewed
  5. View information on the right
