System Administration

Creating a New User

  1. Click on Users tab
  2. Click the plus sign near the search bar
  3. Fill in user email
  4. Click "Create User"

Managing User Roles

  1. Click on Users tab
  2. Choose a user
  3. Toggle System Permissions and User Settings in the far right section

Managing User Roles at Client Level

  1. Click on Users tab
  2. Choose a user
  3. Click on Clients tab
  4. Click on the Client card
  5. Toggle Client/User Roles in far right section

Suspending Users

Adding Users to a Client

  1. Click on Clients tab
  2. Choose a Client
  3. Click on Users tab
  4. Click on plus sign next to users search bar
  5. Add the users email address
  6. Click "Add User"

Suspending Content

Creating a Profit Center

  1. Click on Profit Center tab
  2. Click on the plus sign next to the searh bar for Profit Center
  3. Fill in new Profit Center information
  4. Click on "Create Profit Center"

Adding Authorized Users to a Profit Center

  1. Click on Profit Center tab
  2. Choose a Profit Center to add an authorized user to
  3. Click on Authorized Users tab
  4. Click on the plus sign next to the Authorized Users search bar
  5. Input the authorized user's email address
  6. Click on "Add User"

Viewing Clients in a Profit Center

  1. Click on Profit Center tab
  2. Choose a Profit Center to view Client information
  3. Click on Clients tab
