  2. Choose a user

Creating a Profit Center

  1. Click on Profit Center tab
  2. Click on the plus sign next to the searh bar for Profit Center
  3. Fill in new Profit Center information
  4. Click on "Create Profit Center"

Adding Authorized Users to a Profit Center

  1. Click on Profit Center tab
  2. Choose a Profit Center to add an authorized user to
  3. Click on Authorized Users tab
  4. Click on the plus sign next to the Authorized Users search bar
  5. Input the authorized user's email address
  6. Click on "Add User"
