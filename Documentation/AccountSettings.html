Updating Your Information

expand_more
  1. Click to select each field that is to be updated and enter the updated information
  2. Whenever any field is changed, Update Account and Discard Changes buttons will be available at the bottom of the form. To save your changes, click Update Account

Changing Your Password

expand_more
  1. Enter your current password in the Current Password field
  2. Enter your desired new password in the New Password and Confirm Password fields
  3. Click Update Account at the bottom of the form to complete the update
  4. The new password will be checked for MAP password requirements

If you sign in to Milliman Access Portal using credentials controlled by your organization, please contact your local IT department for help updating your password.
