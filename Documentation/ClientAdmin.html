Client Administration

Creating a New Client

  1. Press the large “New Client” button on the left side of the screen. Or, press the small “+” button to the right of the “Filter Clients” text box. A form will open allowing for client information to be input.
    1. A Client Name must be provided
    2. A Profit Center must be chosen in order to create a new Client
  2. Press “Create Client” button to create the Client

Creating a Sub-Client

  1. Press the green plus sign on the Client card that you want to create a Sub-Client for
  2. Follow the steps above for creating a new Client

Editing Client Information

  1. Select the Client and either click on the blue pencil on the card, or click on the pencil in the upper right corner of the form to edit Client details
  2. Select the desired text box and input the updated information

Deleting a Client

  1. Click on the red trash icon on the card of the Client to delete
  2. Press “Delete”
  3. Enter password to confirm deletion and press “Delete”

Adding Users to a Client

  1. Click on the Client card that you wish to add users to
  2. Click on the plus icon in the upper right corner of the furthest right column
  3. Enter the users email address in the Add User pop up (Note that the domain of the user’s email address must be in the Approved Email Domain List for the specified Client)

Adjusting User Roles

  1. Click on a user card within the Client
  2. Click on the down blue arrow on the user’s card
  3. Toggle the user’s roles to turn off or on

Removing a User

  1. Click on the user card you wish to remove
  2. Click on the minus icon on the card to remove the user
