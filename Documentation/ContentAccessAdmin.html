Content Access Administration

Creating a Selection Group

  1. Choose a Client by clicking the card
  2. Choose a content item by clicking the card
  3. Under Selection Groups press the plus button near the search bar or click on the card that says “ADD SELECTION GROUP”
  4. Enter a Selection Group name
  5. Click “Add Group”

Managing Users in Selection Groups

  1. Navigate to the Selection Group
  2. Click the blue down arrow on the selection group card to view users
  3. Click the blue pencil to edit the group name/users information

Adding Users in Selection Groups

  1. While in edit mode on the expanded card, type in the user’s email address and press the green plus sign
  2. When finished adding users, press the green checkmark on the card to save changes

Removing Users in Selection Groups

  1. While in edit mode on the expanded card, press the red minus sign next to the users name to remove
  2. When finished removing users, press the green checkmark on the card to save changes

Managing Access to Content

  1. Navigate and select the Selection Group card
  2. Make selections in the Selections column to the far right

Suspending Access For a Selection Group

  1. Navigate and select the Selection Group card
  2. Click the toggle for “Suspend Access”
