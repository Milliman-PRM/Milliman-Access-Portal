Creating a Selection Group

expand_more
  1. Choose the Client for the content item you are working on by clicking the client card
  2. Choose the content item you are working on by clicking the content item card
  3. Under Selection Groups press the plus button near the search bar at the top or click on the card that says ADD SELECTION GROUP
  4. Enter a Selection Group name
  5. Click Add Group

Managing Users in Selection Groups

expand_more

Adding Users in Selection Groups

  1. While in edit mode on the expanded card, type in the user’s email address and press the enter or tab buttons to add the user
    • Only users that have been approved by a Client Administrator can be granted access to content
    • A user can only exist in one Selection Group per Content item
  2. When finished adding users, press the green checkmark on the card to save changes

Removing Users in Selection Groups

  1. While in edit mode on the expanded card, press the red minus sign next to the users name to remove
  2. When finished removing users, press the green checkmark on the card to save changes

Managing Access to Content

expand_more
  1. Navigate and select the Selection Group card
  2. For a selection group with access to the master version of the content, click the Unrestricted Access toggle to the ON position
  3. For a selection group with access to a reduced version of the content (only applicable for reducible content):
    • Click the Unrestricted Access toggle at the top right to the OFF position
    • Check the desired selections in the Selections column to the right.
  4. For a selection group with access to an editable version of the content (only applicable for Power BI content):
    • Click the Editable toggle at the top right to the ON position
    • Note: This option will only be available if the content item is published as 'editable' by a Content Publisher.

Suspending Access For a Selection Group

expand_more
  1. Navigate and select the desired Selection Group card
  2. Click the Suspend Access toggle to the ON position
