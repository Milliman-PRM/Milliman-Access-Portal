## Creating a New Content Item
  1. Select a client under the "Client Tree" column on the left side of the screen
  1. Click on the large "Add Root Content Item" button and fill out the Content Item form

## Updating Existing Content
  1. Select the client under the "Client Tree"
  1. Select the content item to be updated. A form will open allowing
  for that item's information to be updated
