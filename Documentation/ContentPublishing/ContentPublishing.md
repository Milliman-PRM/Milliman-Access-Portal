## Creating a New Content Item
1. Select a client under the **Client Tree** column on the left side of the screen
1. Click on the plus sign next to the search bar or click on the large "Add Root Content Item" button 
1. Fill out the Content Item form
1. Click "Create Content Item"

### Publishing a New Content Item
1. After the virus scan has complete, click the blue checkmark on the Content Item card to "Go Live"
1. Check off each section if it is correct
1. Click Approve

## Updating Existing Content
1. Select the client under the **Client Tree**
1. Select the content item to be updated
1. Click on green publishing icon on the content card or click on the publish icon in the upper right corner
1. Update the form on the right side of the screen
1. Click "Republish"

## Deleting Content Items
1. Navigate to the Content Item to be deleted
1. Click on the red trash icon
1. Press "Delete"
1. Enter password to confirm deletion and press "Delete"
