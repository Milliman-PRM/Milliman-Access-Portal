## Updating Your Information
1. Select the text field of each attribute that is to be updated, and enter the
updated information into each of the fields.
1. Once any information is updated, options to "Update Account" or "Discard Changes" will be displayed below the form. To submit the update, just select "Update Account."

## Changing Your Password
1. Enter your current password along with your new password
in the corresponding text fields. You will need to confirm your new password as well.
1. Select the option "Update Account," which will be displayed below the text fields.
